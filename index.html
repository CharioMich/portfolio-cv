Started my journey in the marine industry with hands-on experience navigating and maintaining commercial vessels, ensuring safe, on-time cargo delivery around the world. However, after succesfully graduating from the Merchant Marine Academy, I found myself in need of some more developing and creative enviroments. My passion for technology and constant growth led me ashore into the world of code.

Under my sister's (computer engineer) guidance, I started learning how to code, utilizing various online learning resources such as Harvard's CS50 course, freeCodeCamp and CodeAcademy before I eventually started studying at AUEB's top-tier CS program, Coding Factory. Ever since, I started developing full-stack applications using JavaScript, Java, SQL... aiming to solve real-world problems.

Previous roles at Inditex and as a barista have sharpened my communicational skills, customer-centered mindset and adaptability under pressure.
Now, I seek to make my first steps into the tech world, bringing a unique blend of discipline, attention to detail, teamwork and most importantly, willingness to learn and grow. Hoping to get my first developer or relevant role position where I can leverage my operational rigor and newly minted coding skills.
